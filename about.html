Hospital Introduction

St. Johns Hospital is a modern healthcare facility dedicated to providing high-quality and compassionate medical services. We offer outpatient, inpatient, maternity, emergency, laboratory, and pharmacy services to ensure complete healthcare solutions for our community.

Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare services with compassion and professionalism.

Vision

To be a leading healthcare institution known for excellence and patient-centered care.

Core Values

Integrity, Compassion, Teamwork, Excellence, Accountability.

Hospital History

2010

St. Johns Hospital was established as a small outpatient clinic.

2013

Expanded to include inpatient and maternity services.

2016

Introduced laboratory and radiology services.

2019

Upgraded emergency department and operating theatre.

2022

Implemented electronic medical records system.
